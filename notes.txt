Clock
- now the clock is incremented only by a replica every time it receives a request from some of the clients.
- The internal message exchanges between the replica do not increase the clock value.

2PC
- The coordinator sends an update message to all the replicas with the clock value.
- It adds the entry in a map where the key is the clock and the value the amount of ACK received.
- When it receives more than half ACK it sends back WRITEOK with the clock stored in the HashMap as key
